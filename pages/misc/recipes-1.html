1. Chana Masala (Chickpea Curry)

Serves: 6 (as a side curry with other dishes) Prep + cook time: ~45 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (400 g each) cooked chickpeas, drained and rinsed (~2 cups)
  • 1.5 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped (or 1 small can 400 g chopped tomatoes)
  • 2 green chilies, slit (optional, adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1.5 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1.5 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup water
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2–3 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown (~8 min).
  3. Stir in garlic, ginger, and green chilies; sauté 1–2 min.
  4. Add turmeric, coriander, cumin, and chili powder; cook 30 seconds.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, cook 5–7 min until soft and saucy.
  6. Add chickpeas, salt, and 1 cup water. Stir and simmer 15–20 min until slightly thickened and well-flavored.
  7. Add garam masala and lemon juice; mix well.
  8. Garnish with cilantro. Serve hot with rice.

2. Red Lentil Dal (Masoor Dal)

Serves: 6 (as a side curry) Prep + cook time: ~35 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups red lentils (masoor dal), rinsed
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped (or 1 small can 200 g)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ¾ tsp coriander powder
  • ¼–½ tsp chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 3 cups water or vegetable stock
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1–2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Rinse lentils thoroughly until water runs clear; set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Add mustard and cumin seeds; fry 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden (~5 min). Add garlic and ginger; sauté 1–2 min.
  4. Stir in turmeric, coriander, chili powder, and chopped tomato; cook 5 min until tomato softens.
  5. Add rinsed lentils and 3 cups water/stock; bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer 15–20 min, stirring occasionally, until lentils are soft and soup-like. Adjust consistency with extra water if too thick.
  7. Season with salt. Stir in lemon juice and garnish with cilantro. Serve hot with rice.
